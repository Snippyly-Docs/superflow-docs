## How to Invite Admin Users

In this article, you will learn how to invite admin users to a project.

1. Find the **Superflow Toolbar** and click the **More Options** icon.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-access-project-settings/how-to-access-project-settings/INT1FINAL.png)
</Frame>

2. A sub-menu will expand, click the **View Project** option to select.

3. A new tab will direct you to the **Project Dashboard** page.

> **Note**: By default, you will be directed to the Dashboard for Comment Status screen when accessing the **Project Dashboard**.

4. Click the **USERS** module to access the **Users** page.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-invite-admin-users/IU1FINAL.png)
</Frame>

5. Find and click the **+ Invite Users** button.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-invite-admin-users/IU2FINAL.png)
</Frame>

6. The **Invite Users** dialog box will appear. In the **Email** field, enter the userâ€™s email address.

7. Click the **Drop-Down** menu and select **Admin**.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-invite-admin-users/IU3FINAL.png)
</Frame>

8. After entering and defining the required credentials, click the **Send invites** button.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-invite-admin-users/IU4FINAL.png)
</Frame>
