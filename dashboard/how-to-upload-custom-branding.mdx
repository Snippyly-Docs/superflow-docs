## How to Upload Custom Branding

In this article, you will learn how to apply custom branding to your Superflow account.

Access the Superflow Dashboard by going to [app.usesuperflow.com](app.usesuperflow.com)app.usesuperflow.com). Alternatively, when you are in Review Mode, you can use the method below:

1. Find the **Superflow Toolbar** and click the **More Options** icon.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-access-project-settings/how-to-access-project-settings/INT1FINAL.png)
</Frame>

2. A sub-menu will expand, click the **View Project** option to select.

3. A new tab will direct you to the **Project Dashboard** page.

> **Note**: By default, you will be directed to the Dashboard for Comment Status screen when accessing the **Project Dashboard**.

4. Click the **PREFERENCES** module to access the branding **Custom Branding** page.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-upload-custom-branding/CB1FINAL.png)
</Frame>

5. There are two (2) branding sections that you can customize; (a) the **Superflow Toolbar Logo** and (b) the **Portal Logo**.

6. In this example, we will customize the **Superflow Toolbar Logo**. Go to the **Upload Superflow Toolbar Logo here** panel and click the **Upload Logo** button.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-upload-custom-branding/CB2FINAL.png)
</Frame>

7. The **File Location** window will appear, find and select the **Image File** that you want to upload.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-upload-custom-branding/CB3FINAL.png)
</Frame>

8. Click **Open** to continue uploading the selected file.

To upload an image that will be used on the portal, follow the steps above but go to the **Upload the Logo to be used on the Portal** panel and click its **Upload Logo** button.
