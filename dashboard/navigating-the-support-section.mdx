## Navigating the Support Section

In this article, you will learn how to navigate to the Support section, access useful help topics, and/or reach out to one of our support agents.

Access the Superflow Dashboard by going to app.usesuperflow.com. Alternatively, when you are in Review Mode, you can use the method below:

1. Find the **Superflow Toolbar** and click the **More Options** icon.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/how-to-access-project-settings/how-to-access-project-settings/INT1FINAL.png)
</Frame>

2. A sub-menu will expand, click the **View Project** option to select.

3. A new tab will direct you to the **Project Dashboard** page.

> **Note**: By default, you will be directed to the Dashboard for Comment Status screen when accessing the **Project Dashboard**.

4. Find and click the **Contact Support** icon on the bottom right of the screen.

> **Note**: alternatively, you can click the **Contact Support** module.

<Frame>
![](/images/dashboard/navigating-the-support-section/SUP1FINAL.png)
</Frame>

5. The **Support** window will expand. In this window you can do the following:

- **Messages** - Send us a message using this option.
- **Help** - Access the Help option where you can search for help articles.
- **Send us a Message**
- **Docs** - Directs you to the How-To Guides and FAQs page.
- **Install Videos** -  Directs you to the Superflow YouTube channel where you can find the Superflow Install Guides.
- **Book Demo** - This directs you to Superflowâ€™s Calendly scheduler app where you can book for a demo.
