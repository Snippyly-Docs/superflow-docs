## 1) Select Shopify from the list of Platforms

Choose Shopify from the list of options to install Superflow with Shopify.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/select-shopify-final.png)
</Frame>

## 2) Enter Your Site URL

Enter the site URL you want Superflow to be installed in.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/enter-url-final.png)
</Frame>

## 3) Copy your unique Superflow code

You can copy your unique Superflow code from the onboarding flow or from your project settings in your dashboard.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/copy-code-final.png)
</Frame>

## 4) Go to the Themes setting on your Webflow Site and edit the code.

You can get to the Themes setting at the bottom left of the side menu. Then next to the Customize button, click the ... button and then click Edit Code.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/code-shopify-final.png)
</Frame>



## 5) Add your unique Superflow code in the Footer section

Click `Sections` on the side menu and then click on the `footer.liquid` file.

Next, do a ctrl F search for `</footer>`. 

You should paste your Superflow code snippet right above the `</footer>` tag. 

Be sure to save.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/footer-code-final.png)
</Frame>


## 6) Verify your Installation

Verify your Superflow installation by clicking the Verify button on the onboarding flow.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/verify-final.png)
</Frame>

If successful, you should be redirected to your published Shopify site and see the following success message:

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/verified-final.png)
</Frame>

## 7) Troubleshooting

Having trouble getting your verifying your installation? Here are the common mistakes users encounter when installing Superflow:

 - Did you forget to save the Custom Code?
 - Did you forget to publish your site?
 - Is the browser still loading a cached version?
 - Did you add `?review=true` at the end of your url?
 - Did you publish your changes on a staging URL while you are trying to access a Prod URL?
 - Did you accidentally install multiple Superflow scripts in the Custom Code?


## 8) Navigate to your Shopify site 

Open you have verified that Superflow has been installed on your Shopify site, navigate to your published site and add `?review=true` at the end of the URL to enable Superflow. 

`Example: mywebsite.shopify.com/?review=true`

If you are unsure, what your Shopify site URL is, you can click the Preview button at the top right of the Themes settings page.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/preview-shopify-final.png)
</Frame>
