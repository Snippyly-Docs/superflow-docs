## 1) Select Squarespace from the list of Platforms

Choose Squarespace from the list of options to install Superflow with Squarespace.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/select-squarespace-final.png)
</Frame>

## 2) Enter Your Site URL

Enter the site URL you want Superflow to be installed in.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/enter-url-final.png)
</Frame>

## 3) Copy your unique Superflow code

You can copy your unique Superflow code from the onboarding flow or from your project settings in your dashboard.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/copy-code-final.png)
</Frame>

## 4) Go to Settings on your Squarespace Site

You can get to Settings by clicking on the the bottom left of the side menu.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/settings-squarespace-1-final.png)
</Frame>

## 5) Go to Developer Tools -> Code Injection within Settings
Next, click on Developer Tools on the bottom left, and after the dropdown expands, click on Code Injection.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/settings-squarespace-2-final.png)
</Frame>

## 6) Add your unique Superflow code in the Footer section

Scroll down and add your unique Superflow code in the Footer Code section. Be sure to save.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/footer-squarespace-final.png)
</Frame>

## 7) Publish your Squarespace site

To Publish your site and make it live to the public, first go to Settings by clicking on the bottom left of the side menu.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/settings-squarespace-1-final.png)
</Frame>

Next, click on Site Availability and change it to Public and then press Publish.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/publish-squarespace-final.png)
</Frame>


## 8) Verify your Installation

Verify your Superflow installation by clicking the Verify button on the onboarding flow.

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/verify-final.png)
</Frame>

If successful, you should be redirected to your published Squarespace site and see the following success message:

<Frame>
![](/getting-started/images/verified-final.png)
</Frame>

## 9) Troubleshooting

Having trouble getting your verifying your installation? Here are the common mistakes users encounter when installing Superflow:

 - Did you forget to save the Custom Code?
 - Did you forget to publish your site?
 - Is the browser still loading a cached version?
 - Did you add `?review=true` at the end of your url?
 - Did you publish your changes on a staging URL while you are trying to access a Prod URL?
 - Did you accidentally install multiple Superflow scripts in the Custom Code?


## 10) Navigate to your Squarespace site 

Open you have verified that Superflow has been installed on your Squarespace site, navigate to your publsihed site and add `?review=true` at the end of the URL to enable Superflow. 

`Example: mywebsite.squarespace.com/?review=true`

You should now see a Superflow toolbar on the bottom right of you screen.
