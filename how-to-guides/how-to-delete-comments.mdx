## How to Delete Comments

In this article, you will learn how to delete existing comments.

1. On the screen, find and click the **Comment** you want to delete.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/how-to-delete-comments/DC1FINAL.png)
</Frame>

2. The **Comment Box** will appear, click the **Menu** (...) to enable more options.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/how-to-delete-comments/DC2FINAL.png)
</Frame>

3. Click **Delete Thread**. A **Confirmation** dialog box will appear. Click **Delete** to continue deleting the comment.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/how-to-delete-comments/DC3FINAL.png)
</Frame>
