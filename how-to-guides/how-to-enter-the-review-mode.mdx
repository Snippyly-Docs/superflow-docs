## How to Enter the Review Mode

In this article, you will learn how to enter the Review Mode and enable the Superflow Toolbar.

1. Open a **Browser** and go to the page you want to review.



2. In the **Address Bar**, edit the address by adding this value at the end of the URL: **?review=true**.



3. Press **Enter** on your keyboard to enable the **Review Mode**.

4. You will enter the **Review Mode** and the **Superflow Toolbar** will be enabled.
