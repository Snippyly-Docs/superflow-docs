## How to Leave Video Comments

In this article, you will learn how to leave video comments using the Superflow Toolbar.

1. Find the **Superflow Toolbar** and click the **Comment** icon.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/)
</Frame>

2. After clicking the icon, click the **Element** you want to comment on.

> **Note**: when you hover your cursor on an element, it will appear as a **Comment** icon which signifies that you selected the comment feature.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/)
</Frame>

3.  The **Comment Box** will appear, click the **Camera** icon to select it.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/)
</Frame>

4. A **Recording** window will appear. Click the **Start Recording** button to begin recording the video comment.


<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/)
</Frame>

5. To complete your recording, click the **Red Stop Recording** button to stop the current recording.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/)
</Frame>

6. You can add a comment or note in the **Comment** field and click the **Post** icon to save the video comment.


<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/)
</Frame>
