## How to Search Comments

In this article, you will learn how to use the Search Bar to narrow down your comment search.

1. Find the **Superflow Toolbar** and click the **Open Comments** icon.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/)
</Frame>

2. The **Comments Sidebar** opens. On the top of the **Comments Sidebar**, find and click the **Search comments** field.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/)
</Frame>

3. In the **Search comments** field, enter the comment keyword.

<Frame>
![](/images/how-to-guides/)
</Frame>

4. When you start typing the keywords, the **Comments Sidebar** automatically displays related search results.
