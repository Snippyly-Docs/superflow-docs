## 1) Select Other from the list of Platforms

Choose Other from the list of options to install Superflow with Elementor.

<Frame>
![](/images/select-other-final.png)
</Frame>

## 2) Enter Your Site URL

Enter the site URL you want Superflow to be installed in.

<Frame>
![](/images/enter-url-final.png)
</Frame>

## 3) Copy your unique Superflow code

You can copy your unique Superflow code from the onboarding flow or from your project settings in your dashboard.

<Frame>
![](/images/copy-code-final.png)
</Frame>

## 4) Go to your Wordpress Dashboard, then click on Custom Code under Elementor 

Go to your Wordpress Dashboard then click on Elementor in the side navigation. 

Next, click on Custom Code and then click Create New Custom Code to add your unique Superflow code

<Frame>
![](/images/settings-elementor-final.png)
</Frame>

## 5) Add Your unique Superflow Code

Create a new Custom Code title and paste in your unique Superflow Code

<Frame>
![](/images/code-elementor-add-final.png)
</Frame>

Be sure to change the location to `<Body> - End`.

<Frame>
![](/images/code-elementor-body.png)
</Frame>


## 5) Publish your Elementor site


Click the Publish button at the right of the page to publish your Elementor site.

<Frame>
![](/images/publish-elementor-final.png)
</Frame>



## 6) Verify your Installation

Verify your Superflow installation by clicking the Verify button on the onboarding flow.

<Frame>
![](/images/verify-final.png)
</Frame>

If successful, you should be redirected to your published Elementor site and see the following success message:

<Frame>
![](/images/verified-final.png)
</Frame>

## 7) Troubleshooting

Having trouble getting your verifying your installation? Here are the common mistakes users encounter when installing Superflow:

 - Did you forget to save the Custom Code?
 - Did you forget to publish your site?
 - Is the browser still loading a cached version?
 - Did you add `?review=true` at the end of your url?
 - Did you publish your changes on a staging URL while you are trying to access a Prod URL?
 - Did you accidentally install multiple Superflow scripts in the Custom Code?


## 8) Navigate to your Elementor site 

Open you have verified that Superflow has been installed on your Elementor site, navigate to your publsihed site and add `?review=true` at the end of the URL to enable Superflow. 

`Example: mywebsite.Elementor.io/?review=true`

You should now see a Superflow toolbar on the bottom right of you screen.
