## 1) Select Wix from the list of Platforms

Choose Wix from the list of options to install Superflow with Wix.

<Frame>
![](/images/select-wix-final.png)
</Frame>

## 2) Enter Your Site URL

Enter the site URL you want Superflow to be installed in.

<Frame>
![](/images/enter-url-final.png)
</Frame>

## 3) Copy your unique Superflow code

You can copy your unique Superflow code from the onboarding flow or from your project settings in your dashboard.

<Frame>
![](/images/copy-code-final.png)
</Frame>

## 4) Go to Settings on your Wix Site and then click Custom Code

You can get to Settings at the bottom left of the side menu. Then scroll down and click Custom Code.

<Frame>
![](/images/code-wix-final.png)
</Frame>


## 5) Add your unique Superflow code in the Body - End section

Scroll down and add your unique Superflow code in the Body - End section. Be sure to save.

<Frame>
![](/images/footer-wix-final.png)
</Frame>

## 6) Publish your Wix site

To publish your Wix site first go to your home page and then click Edit Site. 

Next, click the publish button in the top right to publish your Wix site.

<Frame>
![](/images/publish-wix-final.png)
</Frame>


## 7) Verify your Installation

Verify your Superflow installation by clicking the Verify button on the onboarding flow.

<Frame>
![](/images/verify-final.png)
</Frame>

If successful, you should be redirected to your published Wix site and see the following success message:

<Frame>
![](/images/verified-final.png)
</Frame>

## 8) Troubleshooting

Having trouble getting your verifying your installation? Here are the common mistakes users encounter when installing Superflow:

 - Did you forget to save the Custom Code?
 - Did you forget to publish your site?
 - Is the browser still loading a cached version?
 - Did you add `?review=true` at the end of your url?
 - Did you publish your changes on a staging URL while you are trying to access a Prod URL?
 - Did you accidentally install multiple Superflow scripts in the Custom Code?


## 9) Navigate to your Wix site 

Open you have verified that Superflow has been installed on your Wix site, navigate to your publsihed site and add `?review=true` at the end of the URL to enable Superflow. 

`Example: mywebsite.wix.com/?review=true`


## 10) Test out Superflow

You should now see a Superflow toolbar on the bottom right of you screen.
