# Superflow Product Updates - Feb 15, 2024

We are excited to announce the following features and improvements to enhance your workflow. Here's what's new:


## Minimap

Introducing the Minimap feature! Now you can effortlessly navigate through your comments by viewing a concise overview located on the right-hand side of the page. Easily locate and jump to specific sections with ease.

## Device Info

Gain insights into your collaborators' devices with the Device Info feature. Now, alongside each comment, you can see the type of device used to leave the comment, whether it's Mobile or Desktop.

## Filters for Archived Projects

Managing archived projects just got easier! You can now set the filter to display archived projects directly within your dashboard. Keep track of past projects effortlessly and access them whenever needed.
